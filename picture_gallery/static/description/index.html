Website Picture Gallery

Picture Gallery to present your photos on the odoo website

Manage your picture galleries on an easy way and present your beautifull images to your visitors. The picture gallery is shown on the odoo website als new menu "Picture Gallery" and all your photos are categoriesed in clear galleries.

Photos in galleries are shown with a slider which is runnin automatically. Manually scrolling can be done with arrows on the left or right side.

With the fullscreen mode, everybody can view your beautiful pictures in a hugh size.

Creating a new gallery in the backend is very easy. You only have to enter a gallery description and some optional informations.

Into your gallery you put your pictures. You can upload a single photo to your gallery. Attributes as filename and resulation are taken automatically from the uploaded image. The image and all information are stored in your Odoo database.

If you have to upload more than one photo, a mass upload is available. With button "Upload Images" you can select multiple images and upload them in one step.
